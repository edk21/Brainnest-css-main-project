About The Eternals

The Eternals is a 2021 American superhero film directed by Chlo√© Zhao.

It is the twenty-sixth film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and the third in Phase IV. It features the Eternals characters, created by Jack Kirby.

In the beginning, before the six Singularities (which became the Infinity Stones) and the dawn of the Big Bang, ppeared the Celestials. Arishem, the Prime Celestial, created the first star and implanted Celestials in carrier planets across the Universe. For its development, a Celestial needs a considerable amount of energy from intelligent lives of its carrier planet. Each new billion years marks the "Emergence" of a Celestial and each of them creates a star thanks to the energy it has accumulated from its carrier planet, which generates gravity, heat and light necessary for the formation of a new galaxy. Arishem subsequently created the Deviants, so that they rid the carrier planets of their predators and thus ensure the development of intelligent life there. But they escaped his control by becoming predators themselves on these planets.
